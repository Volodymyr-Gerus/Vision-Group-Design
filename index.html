ABOUT US

Vision Group Design

Vision Group Design has been designing interiors and exteriors since 2017. We have formed teams of narrow-profile craftsmen who perform work of various levels of complexity, from disassembly to final decoration and installation of furniture.

Vision Group Design has completed more than 300 individual projects for our clients and implemented more than 150 projects. Our clients are also international companies, government agencies and private companies.

The company's employees are always focused on high-quality and clear performance of tasks in order for our client to be satisfied with the result.
